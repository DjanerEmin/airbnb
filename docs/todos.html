Things to do

I am starting a list of locations for you to visit while in Dresden. I will focus on Neustadt, where all the cool bars, cafes and clubs are located.

⌘ Points of Interest

🌹 Rosengarten

A beautifully landscaped rose garden along the Elbe River, featuring around 100 different types of roses and various sculptures. Open year-round with free admission.

📍 Carusufer 12, 01099 Dresden

📍 Google Maps

⛪ Martin-Luther-Platz & Martin-Luther-Kirche

A picturesque square surrounded by Gründerzeit architecture, centered around the neo-Gothic Martin Luther Church, which plays a significant role in the city’s cultural life.

📍 Martin-Luther-Platz 5, 01099 Dresden

📍 Google Maps

🌳 Alaunpark

A spacious urban park popular among locals for relaxation, picnics, and outdoor activities. It features playgrounds, sports facilities, and open green spaces.

📍 Bischofsweg, 01099 Dresden

📍 Google Maps

🐎 Goldener Reiter

An iconic gilded equestrian statue of Augustus the Strong, symbolizing Dresden’s rich history. Located at the Neustädter Markt, it’s a prominent landmark in the city.

📍 Hauptstraße 38, 01097 Dresden

📍 Google Maps

⛲ Albertplatz

A central square in Neustadt known for its impressive fountains and as a major tram interchange connecting various parts of the city.

📍 Albertplatz, 01097 Dresden

📍 Google Maps

🎭 Scheune Vorplatz

An open space in front of the Scheune cultural center, often used for public events, markets, and gatherings. It’s a vibrant spot reflecting the district’s artistic spirit.

📍 Alaunstraße 36-40, 01099 Dresden

📍 Google Maps

🪦 Innerer Neustädter Friedhof

One of Dresden’s oldest cemeteries, notable for its baroque grave monuments and as the final resting place for many of the city’s historical figures.

📍 Friedensstraße 2, 01097 Dresden

📍 Google Maps
